Digital Arrest Scam — Awareness Summary
Fraudsters impersonate police, CBI, ED, or Customs officials over video call,
claiming the victim is involved in a crime (e.g. a parcel containing drugs).
They pressure the victim to stay on camera continuously ("digital arrest")
and transfer money to "clear their name." Real law enforcement agencies do
not conduct arrests or investigations over video call, and never ask for
money transfers to avoid arrest. If you receive such a call, disconnect and
report immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
