OTP Scam — Awareness Summary
Never share an OTP with anyone over phone, SMS, or call, even if the caller
claims to be from your bank, a delivery company, or a government office.
Banks never ask for OTPs to "verify" or "reverse" a transaction. If you
shared an OTP and notice unauthorized activity, report it immediately to
your bank and at cybercrime.gov.in.
